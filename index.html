🔬 ML METHODOLOGY

How the ML Detection Works

A transparent, explainable-AI pipeline. Every decision is traceable back to extracted domain features.

01
📝

Feature Extraction

20+ lexical, structural, and statistical features extracted from the raw domain — entropy, length, digit ratio, vowel ratio, consonant clusters, n-gram analysis, and more.

→
02
⚖️

Weighted Scoring

Each feature is multiplied by a trained weight derived from PhishTank, SURBL, and DGA detector research datasets. Uses a logistic-regression-style decision boundary.

→
03
🏷

Module Classification

Six specialized detectors run in parallel: Phishing Keywords, Malware/DGA, TLD Risk Score, Nepali Brand Impersonation, Typosquatting (Levenshtein), and Structural Anomaly.

→
04
🧠

AI Deep Analysis

All extracted features and scores are sent to Claude AI which provides contextual threat intelligence, Nepal-specific risk context, and actionable user recommendations.

📊

Shannon Entropy

Measures character randomness. High entropy (>3.8) indicates algorithmically generated (DGA) abuse domains used by botnets.

H(x) = -Σ p(x)·log₂p(x)
📏

Domain Length

Abuse domains are typically longer. Research shows domains >20 chars have 3× higher abuse rate. Weighted feature in ML model.

len("nabilbank-secure") = 16
🔢

Digit Ratio

High digit-to-letter ratio signals DGA or spam infrastructure. Legitimate brands almost never use digits in their base domain.

digits("nc3ll-0ffer") = 0.22
➖

Hyphen Count

Phishers string keywords with hyphens (bank-secure-login-nepal). Multiple hyphens are a top phishing indicator in research.

hyphens("nabil-bank-np") = 2
🔤

Vowel Ratio

Natural English has ~38% vowels. DGA and random-string domains deviate. Key feature in academic DGA detection papers.

vowels("esewa") = 0.60
📐

Levenshtein Distance

Edit-distance algorithm for typosquatting. Within 2 edits of a known brand = impersonation flag in the detector.

dist("g00gle","google") = 2
🔣

Consonant Clusters

Consecutive consonants >4 rarely appear in real words. DGA domains produce unpronounceable sequences like "xkcdstpq".

maxRun("strpqxk") = 7
🌐

TLD Abuse Score

TLDs scored by abuse probability from Spamhaus/SURBL data. .tk (0.97), .ml (0.95), .cf (0.93) — among world's most abused.

abuseScore(".tk") = 0.97
